Welcome to Endangered Animals Guardian Station, a professional platform for world endangered animals popular science. Explore species characteristics, living status, endangered causes and protection measures.

Terrestrial Endangered Animals

Siberian Tiger

One of the largest wild cats, with ~500 individuals left. Threatened by poaching and habitat loss.

Amur Leopard

One of the rarest big cats, with ~100 individuals left. Threatened by habitat loss and poaching.

Sumatran Orangutan

Only ~14,000 left. Critically endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.

Javan Rhinoceros

Only ~80 left in the wild. Critically endangered due to habitat loss and poaching.

Marine Endangered Animals

Bluefin tuna

Highly sought after for sushi, with populations declining due to overfishing.

Hawksbill Turtle

Known for their beautiful shell. Threatened by wildlife trade and fishing gear bycatch.

Vaquita

World's smallest porpoise, <10 individuals left. Endangered by gillnet bycatch.

Blue Whale

Largest animal on Earth. Recovering but still endangered by ship strikes.

Aerial Endangered Animals

Mauritius Kestrel

One of the world's rarest birds, with ~300 individuals left. Threatened by habitat loss.

Philippine Eagle

One of the largest eagles, <500 pairs left. Threatened by deforestation.

California Condor

Once extinct in wild; now ~500 individuals. Recovering via conservation.

Whooping Crane

North America's tallest bird, ~800 left. Threatened by habitat loss.
